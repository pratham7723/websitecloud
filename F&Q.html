Our Return Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility for Returns

  • The item must be unused and in the original packaging.
  • A receipt or proof of purchase is required.

How to Process a Return

  1. Contact our customer service to initiate the return.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Ship the item back using the provided return label.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will notify you via email about the approval or rejection of your refund.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, please contact us at support@example.com.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive an item that is damaged or defective, please contact us immediately at support@example.com. We will offer a full refund or exchange, and we will cover the return shipping costs.

Non-returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable, including:

  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Intimate or sanitary goods

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or damaged. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance for returns over $75.

International Returns

For international orders, the customer is responsible for return shipping fees, as well as any customs duties or taxes. Please ensure that your return is sent using a trackable method to avoid any issues with delivery.

Return Processing Time

Once we receive your returned item, please allow 7-10 business days for the return to be processed. Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method, and exchanges will be shipped within 2-3 business days after processing.
